About

Duendes are small, mythical creatures found in the folklore of various cultures, particularly in Spain and Latin America. They are often described as mischievous beings, sometimes helping humans but other times causing trouble by stealing objects, making noises in the night, or leading travelers astray. Duendes falls into what can be called a "fictitious truth" a narrative that, while not verifiably real, holds cultural significance and remains compelling across generations.
