Memorial

Not all who wander return the same.

There are whispers of names forgotten by time — travelers, children, and skeptics who crossed into the realm of the duendes without knowing the cost. Some vanished without a trace. Others came back... altered, as if shadows clung to their memories.

This memorial honors those souls. Each story etched here is a quiet echo, a caution carried on the wind. They remind us: the unseen is not the unreal, and the forest has ears.

A stone marked by time, standing watch in the woods — no name, just presence.

  • Elena Márquez — Last seen near the hollow cedar. Her laughter still heard on quiet nights.
  • Tomás Delgado — Returned from the mountains, silent and wide-eyed. Spoke only in riddles until his final day.
  • The Rivera Twins — Found asleep in the field with strange symbols traced in the dirt around them.
  • Unnamed Shepherd — Only his whistle was found, lying in a perfect circle of moss.
