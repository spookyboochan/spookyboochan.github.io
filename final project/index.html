They Walk Among Us

Welcome to a world whispered about in fireside stories and half-remembered dreams. A world where shadows move with intent, and ancient spirits wear the faces of tricksters, guardians, and watchers.

Duendes are not myths — they are memories wrapped in moss and bone. Across generations, they’ve crept into legend across Spain, Latin America, and the Philippines. Their names change, but their presence endures.

This site is not here to debate their existence. It is here to document their truth — for those who’ve seen too much… or not quite enough.
