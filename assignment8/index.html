The Beginning

Every meaningful growth begins in darkness, in the quiet depths where we retreat to nurture our strength.

Like seeds beneath the soil, we gather resources, prepare, and wait for the right moment to push upward. This space of apparent stillness is where transformation truly begins.

The internal work done in these moments—reflection, healing, learning—forms the foundation that supports all future expansion.
